The Ye Old Dunn Cow Pub is a classic English pub built in the mid 19th century that's a short drive away form Alton Towers. The pub gives a warm and friendly feeling to those who visit,the pub contains both a idyllic beer garden and a patio area.

The Ye Old Dunn Cow Pub provides a menu of food and bervages for everybody.Also not only is it a classic pub it's also provides rooms for people to stay in were people can enjoy the light and airy rooms with cotton sheets and a television for relaxiation, so why not come and visit to have a great time.
