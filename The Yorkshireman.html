The Yorkshireman is a restaurant on the border between Colton and Rugeley on the Colton road near the train station Rugeley Trent Valley.The restaurant used to be called the Railway Tavern originally and was reopened in 2007.

The restaurant not only provides fine cuisine and beverages, but is also both family friendly and allows dogs in the public bar area called the oak room.
