Hello and welcome to my website about Colton Village, A beautiful traditional Villiage in the heart of the Staffordshire countryside which sits central to Lichfield, Stafford, Uttoxeter and Cannock. The villiage was founded in Iron age times and is mentioned in the doomsday book in 1086.This website will enable you to get a flavour of the villiage of Colton. The villiage contains a varitey of great places you can stay,visit and eat. This website will help show some of them, I hope you enjoy.
