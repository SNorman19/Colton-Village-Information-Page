Built in 1991 Colton Village hall has been the centre of activites in Colton for years, and can hold up to 120 people. The hall contains a bar and kitchen that leads to the main hall which can be used for functions like auctions and party's. The village hall hosts a wide range of activities for everyone such as Tai Chi and the friendship club.These activities give evryone the chance to meet new people and enjoy themselves, plus the village hall also contains a playpark just outside. Along with this is the different organisations that anyone can join, which includes the History socitey, the Colton Produce Guild, Colton W1 and occaisonally the Colton Ramblers.
