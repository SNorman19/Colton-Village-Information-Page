The Ye Olde Dun Cow Pub is a classic English pub built in the mid 19th century that's a short drive away from Alton Towers. The pub gives a warm and friendly feeling to those who visit,and contains both an idyllic beer garden and a patio area.

The Ye Old Dun Cow Pub provides a menu of food and beverages for everybody. Not only is it a classic pub, but it also provides rooms for people to stay in. You can enjoy the light and airy rooms with cotton sheets and a television for relaxiation. So why not come and visit to have a great time.
