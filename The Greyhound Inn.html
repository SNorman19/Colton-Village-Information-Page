A classic village pub that sits in the village of Colton, the Greyhound date's back to the 19th-century, and was refurbished in the mid-1980s.The attractive bar features a brick-lined fire-place and red tiled floor, while the comfortable lounge, with some high wood-backed seeting, has a similar homely feel.

The Greyhound contains tradional pubs game like darts and crib.The Greyhound occasionally hosts live music for people to enjoy. The Greyhound allows dogs by the public bar only and the pub is family freindly but children can only stay till 7:30pm.There is a pub garden outside by the car park so people can have a nice drink outside as well.
