Built in 1853 St Mary's church remains the only church in Colton. The church has close connections with the local community with the local school sharing the same name. It's best known for being popular for both baptisms and weddings.The building has been used for all types of events such as concerts, new-year meals, tree festivals, youth club, and quiet prayer, as well as being used for worship.
